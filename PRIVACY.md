# Privacy Policy

Stream Pad is a local desktop app. It stores your profiles, buttons, icons, and
actions on your own computer.

Stream Pad does not run its own analytics service and does not sell personal
data.

The app may connect to the internet when you use features that need it:

- Check for Updates contacts the GitHub Releases endpoint for this project.
- Share Feedback opens the configured Google Forms feedback page in your browser.
- Website drops may use Google's favicon service to fetch a website icon.

Files, folders, apps, URLs, and sounds that you assign to buttons are stored in
the local app configuration so the app can run those actions later.

If you submit feedback through Google Forms, that submission is handled by
Google's services and the form owner's Google account.
